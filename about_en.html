Turku Tutuksi - Culture at your fingertips

"Ducunt volentem fata, nolentem trahunt - the fates lead the willing and drag the unwilling"

Turku tutuksi is a service for tourists and residents of Turku region alike that are interested in cultural sights. TT gathers all the interesting cultural sights on to a one map for easy access.

Open data

About the usage of open data on this site

Turku Tutuksi is built with the help of open data. This enables us to provide a reliable, unbribable and up-to-date service for all page users.

Lounaistieto
Regional information service operating in Southwest Finland: Locations of cultural sigths
OpenStreetMap
Open map service managed by the OpenStreetMap community.
Leaflet
Open source JavaScript library. Responsible for the data visible on the map.
